# My Favorite Movie: Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them

> *"My philosophy is that worrying means you suffer twice." — Newt Scamander*

## Overview
**Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them** is a fantasy film written by **J.K. Rowling**, set in the Wizarding World before Harry Potter’s time.  
It follows **Newt Scamander**, a magizoologist who travels to New York with a case full of magical creatures.

## Main Characters
- **Newt Scamander** (Eddie Redmayne)
- **Tina Goldstein** (Katherine Waterston)
- **Queenie Goldstein** (Alison Sudol)
- **Jacob Kowalski** (Dan Fogler)
- **Gellert Grindelwald** (Johnny Depp)

## Why I Love It
1. Magical creatures and adventures
2. Amazing visual effects
3. The story expands the Harry Potter universe
4. Newt’s kindness and bravery
